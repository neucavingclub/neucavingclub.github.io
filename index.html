Our major news

Near East University Caving Club is one of the quickly developing societies in the University.

Become a Member

Join us and feel what it means to be part of a great team, it is a decision which is not a soul can regret upon. We provide the basic skills one needs to survive in the wild and also the fun and adventure.

Become big

This society makes everyone involved. This could be a journey to becomes something greater in life. The journey starts with you as a young enthusiast caver ready to explore nature and witness such a natural wonder. A journey to a thousand miles begins with the very first step to take.

Become Volunteer

Be a volunteer in the hiking and cave exploration expedition and camping which gives you a lot of outdoor activities which helps to be part of a team and its teached teamwork and for attaining the set goals. This is a place where we can improve our leadership skills.

We are nonprofit team

Our main aims and objectives are to bring students of all backgrounds and nationalities, train them and educate them about nature conservation and conduct a couple of researches on plants and animals in Cyprus.

We work as a team to learn to trust each other, we learn each other's strength and weaknesses. We about all the caves in Cyprus and our main objective is to learn to use the techniques and apply them in the real caves. One of the reason is to educate the locals of the discovered caves.
